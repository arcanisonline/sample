More about Me

My Childhood

I grew up in a small town on Lake Como - Italy. As a child I was quite good at school and my interests started to grow. In fact, I started playing trumpet in middle school and I was also attracted by computer games and computers in general; however it wasn't untill recent years that I discover my passion for Computer Science as a subject.

My Formal Education

As I said before, I discoverd my interest in Computer Science as a subject only in recent years and my formal education was quite far from it. In fact, during highschool, I studied trumpet with a private teacher and I took formal exams and assessments in Como - Italy. However I managed to graduate in music only later for a couple of reasons: I started having embouchure problems that took years to solve and my interests deviated toward Physics. After highschool, in fact, I studied Physics in Milan - Italy, graduating with full grades. Finally, two years later, I graduated in music, having found again my passion for trumpet playing.

My Job

My current fulltime job is teaching Mathematics in highschool.

Computer Science

Computer science is one of my main interests and I would like to integrate it in my profession. My main area of interest is Artificial Intelligence and Robotics, but I recently decided to learn more about web design and development, so I started this website for learning purposes.

Music

Since graduation, I started playing and teaching trumpet as a semi-professional. I play in local wind bands and, sometimes in local orchestras. Moreover, I recently grew an interest for piano playing. In fact, I'm currently taking piano lessons once a week.
